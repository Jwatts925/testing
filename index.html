About

My work sits between design, construction, and technology. I develop project-specific 3D models, coordinate changing design information, produce shop and proposal graphics, and create dependable workflows for teams working across preconstruction and operations.

With a background in industrial design and English literature, I bring equal attention to how information functions and how it is communicated. That combination helps me translate complicated facade conditions into models, diagrams, schedules, and presentations that are easier to understand and use.
